Our Approach to Developing Solutions

Identify

In the identify phase, the team identifies the core features and functionality of the product. This phase is often iterative, as the team works with stakeholders to refine the product vision.

Implement

In the implement phase, the team builds the product based on the requirements identified in the previous phase. This phase is often linear, as the team works through a set of tasks to build the product.

Iterate

In the iterate phase, the team releases the product to users and collects feedback. This feedback is used to improve the product in the next iteration.
